Introduction

You can use Claude to:

✍️

Write across different formats and styles

Claude can help you draft everything from a text message or an email to a screenplay or a novel.

🎓

Learn something new

Ask Claude to teach you how to make Pasta Carbonara, use a new Python library, explore 20th-century Uruguayan history, or ride a pogo stick.

📝

Summarize text

Claude is great at generating concise summaries of long articles, news stories, documents, conversations, or even books.

💻

Code

Claude can be an extremely helpful 'pair programmer'. While Claude is best at Python, it knows all major programming languages.

💡

Act as a debate or brainstorming partner

Bounce ideas off Claude, generate different viewpoints, get recommendations, and generally converse.

🌐

Translate languages

Claude knows more than a dozen languages and can translate between them. Beyond English, it's especially good at Portuguese, French, and German.

🖼️

Analyze images

Claude can interpret the content of images, helping with things like analyzing chart data or describing what's depicted.
